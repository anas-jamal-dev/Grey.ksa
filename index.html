about us

our location

It would be hard to find friendlier place in Jeddah than this litlle café, but of course it is a matter of taste. This coffee-house is located in two places Tahliya and Hamdania.

what makes our menu special

It is a small café, which specializes in serving coffee and desserts with a small and simple dining room. You can find different types of coffee there - espresso, capuchino, latte, black coffee, coffee with cognac and some others. Also this café provides all kinds of drinks - soft-drinks , such as juice, mineral water and various types of tea. The café’s menu is rather diverse. You can eat salads, fish and meat dishes; tasty cakes, rolls and and pies with different fillings; fruits - apples, oranges, grapefruits and lemons cut in small pieces; sandwiches and pancakes with various syrops and sauces; and a variety of ice-cream, that can be with fruit sweet syrops, nuts and chocolate. The interior of this café is rather cosy and comfortable.
